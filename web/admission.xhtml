UNDERGRADUATE ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS
The minimum qualification for admission into an undergraduate program is:

1. A minimum GPA of 2.5/2nd division/equivalent grade in SSC and HSC or equivalent public examinations.

Students with GPA 2.00 in any examination either SSC or HSC,

however, must have a minimum GPA of 6.00 totalling the GPA of both the examinations.



2. O-level students must have completed a minimum of 5 subjects with Average Grade Point of 2.50

or above and A-level students a minimum of 2 subjects with Average Grade Point of 2.00

or above (scale A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2, E=1)



3. For admission to BSEEE/CSE programs, students must have had Physics and Mathematics in HSC/A-Level or equivalent level.



4. The students, who are sons/daughters of freedom fighters,

will be eligible for getting admission if they have a minimum GPA of 5.00 totalling

the GPA of SSC and HSC Examinations. Such students,

however, have to submit the copy of authenticated Certificate as

the proof of his/her father/mother’s identity as a freedom fighter.

GRADUATE ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS


The minimum qualification for admission into a graduate program is:



1. A relevant undergraduate degree from a recognized university with a GPA of 2.00 or above in a scale of 4.0



2. A relevant Bachelor Degree with at least second divisions/classes in all previous exams.



3. Students completing undergraduate programs from UIU having a minimum CGPA of 2.00 will be eligible

to get admission to any graduate programs without appearing at the Admission Test.

4. Different departments may have some additional requirements.

5. Some students may be required to complete some additional preparatory courses at

UIU before they start their Master’s programs.
